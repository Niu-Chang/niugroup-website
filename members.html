Bio
Dr. Niu’s research interests lie in quantum and topological transport phenomena in chiral materials, as well as nanoelectronics based on emerging channel materials and their integration into next-generation systems. He authored or co-authored over 40 peer-reviewed journal conference papers and received the Best Paper Award at the 2023 IEEE International Electron Devices Meeting (IEDM). He serves as a reviewer for over 10 journals including Physical Review Letters and Nature Communications.
