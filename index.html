Chiraltronics

Our research explores chirality-based topological and quantum transport in chiral materials, where the handedness of electronic states leads to symmetry breaking and novel quantum phenomena.

Device Physics

We investigate low-dimensional semiconductors, electronic transport, and quantum effects toward high-performance electronics.

Materials Engineering & Integration

Thin films, ALD processes, 2D materials, and high-mobility oxide channels for next-generation integration.
